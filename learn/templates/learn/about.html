ABOUT EDUMIND

EDUMIND is a combination of Education and mind, reflecting our mission to empower minds through learning. EDUMIND was founded in 2021 with a mission to make high-qualty education accessible to all learners. It allows learners to have ability to access courses and material on various devices (desktop,mobile,tablet), personalized learning path and recommendations based on progress and performance, tools for communication and collaboration, such as forums,quizzs, messaging, and virtual classroom,interactive and multimedia content such as videos, audio,and simulations. Tne number of users increase from from 10K to 20K in the last three years. EDUMIND is all about providing education and training through digital means, using technology to deliver content, track progress, and facilitate interaction between learners and instructors.
