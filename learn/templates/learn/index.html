About Us

ABOUT EDUMIND

EDUMIND is a combination of Education and mind, reflecting our mission to empower minds through learning. EDUMIND was founded in 2021 with a mission to make high-qualty education accessible to all learners. It allows learners to have ability to access courses and material on various devices (desktop,mobile,tablet), personalized learning path and recommendations based on progress and performance, tools for communication and collaboration, such as forums,quizzs, messaging, and virtual classroom,interactive and multimedia content such as videos, audio,and simulations.

Why Choose Us

Reduced Cost

With EDUMIND, there's no cost to reproduce materials and, thanks to mobile learning and microlearning, learners can use their own tech to participate. And, because the classroom is virtual, you save on travel costs, and instructors can record and distribute their sessions any time.

Learner Satisfaction

Bad attitudes about learning lead to disengagement. Using EDUMIND appeal learners’ hearts and minds by illustrating the “why” behind the learning, and giving learners the chance to see the fruits of their labor via metrics, leaderboards, and certification increases learner satisfaction and promotes a better attitude.

Speed

Who wants to sit through hours of class time to get a few minutes’ worth of new information? EDUMIND speeds up the process for learners who go at a quicker pace, saving time and resources for the ultimate in efficiency. EDUMIND can also allow learners to test out of material they’ve already learned.
